Ziplining

What is Ziplining?

It's an extreme sport activity that involves sliding along steel cables using harnesses and pulleys, typically in a natural setting like forests or mountains. This activity allows participants to enjoy panoramic views and experience an adrenaline rush while moving from one platform to another.

Ziplining Risks

  • Falls from heights
  • Equipment failure
  • Human error
  • Weather conditions
  • Physical injuries
  • Preexisting health conditions
  • Lack of proper supervision
