San Gil Extreme: Passion for Adventure

Discover the most exciting experiences in Colombia's adventure capital

Paragliding

"Fly high, feel the freedom." Experience the adrenaline of paragliding as you soar through the skies and enjoy unforgettable views.

Canoeing

"Conquer the waters, feel the adventure." Paddle with strength and challenge the current while enjoying canoeing in stunning natural landscapes.

Bungee Jumping

"Jump into the void, feel the thrill." Live the adrenaline of bungee jumping and experience free fall at its fullest.

Caving

"Venture into the unknown, explore the depths." Caving takes you on a journey to discover mysterious caves and unique underground formations.

Rappelling

"Descend with courage, conquer the vertical." Rappelling challenges you to descend rocky walls and cliffs, facing gravity while enjoying the thrill of each drop.

Zip Line

"Fly between the treetops, feel the adrenaline of the air." The zip line lets you traverse the heights of the jungle, sliding from platform to platform while enjoying spectacular views.
